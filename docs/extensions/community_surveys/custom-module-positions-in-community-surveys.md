Here are the custom module positions available in survey pages to publish your modules where your template cannot reach.

- surveys-list-above-toolbar
- surveys-list-above-content
- surveys-list-below-pagination
- surveys-category-above-toolbar
- surveys-category-above-categories
- surveys-category-above-content
- surveys-individual-above-toolbar
- surveys-individual-above-content
